History

Madhawa Premaratne started his own practice in 1996. Within his Seventeen years of practice, he has designed a fair number of building projects in various parts of the island. Most are personalized housing while rest are commercial buildings, market centers, hotels restaurants, factory buildings, interior design works etc.


Philosophy

Building up close relationships with cilentele, understanding their aspirations, offering personal attention towards each individual client's problems and give them environment friendly, cost effective, innovative design solutions which will create positive influences their life styles
