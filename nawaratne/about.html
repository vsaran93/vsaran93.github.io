About us

Architect Nishan Nawaratne Is Graduated From The University Of Moratuwa Sri Lanka, And Holding Degree In B.Sc.(Built Environment), M.Sc.(Architecture), And PG.Dip.(Architectural Conservation Of Monuments &Sites).

Archt. Nishan Dhammika Nawaratne, Chartered Architect, Is His Own Establishment, And WSas First Registered In Year 2000. Since Then This Consultancy Practice Has Provided Services To It's Clients Ranging From Personalized, And Mass Housing Developments, Hospitality And Leisure Project Developments, Industrial And Infrastructure Developments, Medical And Healthcare Facility Developments, Agriculture And Floriculture Development Projects, And Various Architectural Conservation Projects Including Projects That Are Located In The World Heritage Site Of Galle Fort.
However, The Key Objective Of The Practice Is In Creating Environmentally Sound, Psychologically Comfortable, And Socially Acceptable Architectural Spaces and Places.

Philosophy

Our Practice Is Focused On Creating Environmentally Sound Socially Acceptable and Psycologically Comfortable Spaces And Places
