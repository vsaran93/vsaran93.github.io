Who we are ?

nexHub is nexmin’s dedicated technology hub, established to design, develop, and maintain the digital ecosystem that drives a modern delivery platform. While nexmin focuses on business operations, partnerships, and customer growth, nexHub ensures that the technology behind the scenes is seamless, secure, and constantly innovating.

Our mission

To provide the digital foundation that enables nexmin to operate smarter, faster, and more sustainably in the food and grocery delivery industry

Our vision

To redefine what’s possible in delivery technology by leveraging AI, automation, and sustainable cloud practices to create the most reliable platform in Europe.
