Senior UI/UX Designer Needed

  • Home
  • jobs
  • Posted On: 1 week ago
  • Post Views: 2500
  • No. of applicant: 30

The Position:

As a Senior UI/UX Designer, the ideal candidate should have experience in website user interface design. Specifically, experience using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to create user-centered layouts, from conceptualization to the production of final assets for development. The Junior UX Designer will work closely with product team members to craft UX artifacts into beautiful hi-fidelity user interface designs—according to business requirements, stakeholder feedback and the needs of our millions of users. No pressure.

The Candidate:

The ideal candidate has:

  • A passion for user interface craftsmanship
  • A keen eye for design aesthetics and UX sensibilities
  • Proven skills in creating user interfaces with Adobe Creative Cloud tools
  • An ability to communicate ideas with stakeholders and design requirements to developers

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with Business Analysts, Development and QA to implement innovative solutions for the product direction, and user experience
  • Contribute to the creation of wireframes, mock-ups, designs, interactions, and interactive prototypes to effectively communicate design ideas
  • Participate in UX design research and documentation
  • Work within a team environment and independently

Must haves:

  • 2+ years of professional design experience
  • Relevant accredited post-secondary education
  • Participate in UX design research and documentation
  • Understanding of current web technology capabilities (HTML, CSS, JS)
  • Excellent English communication skills (written and verbal)
  • Ability to handle feedback well
  • Pixel perfect attention to detail

You must be able to provide proof of legal authorization to work in Canada and for Absorb Software out of the Calgary office.

We look forward to hearing from you!
